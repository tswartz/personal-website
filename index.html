About

I was born to a spunky flamenco dancer and her stoic but tender-hearted bull-fighter husband in Barcelona. We lived above a convent and at night my father played guitar for us while my mother danced under the stars.

Pranked you! I was born in Palo Alto, CA. When I was 6 we moved to Baltimore, MD. Yes, The Wire was filmed there. And yes, I have seen The Wire. Have you seen The Wire? If not, you should.

I grew up loving art and thought for a long that I would pursue that in college and as a career. After I enrolled at Northeastern University, I very suddenly changed my mind and switched my major to Computer Science. I always had an interest in coding and I decided that this was my chance to learn more about it.

BEST. DECISION. EVER.

Northeastern is known for its 5-year "co-op" program in which students complete 2-3 internships in fields related to their majors. As a Computer Science major I have completed 3 totally awesome software-engineering co-ops.

My first one was at Intuit in sunny San Diego, CA. I worked on Turbo Tax Online on the front-end and I ate lunch outside everyday laughing at the thought of my peers shivering through the Boston winter. Just kidding, I ate outside with great respect and sympathy for those in colder climates.

My second co-op was at PowerAdvocate in Boston. I was a full-stack developer on several different products. It was awesome to work with all parts of the application, including front-end, back-end, and testing. I also got the opportunity to be part of a Scrum team where I learned a ton about working as part of a team.

I am currently on my third and final co-op at HubSpot as a front-end developer on the Content Optimization System.
